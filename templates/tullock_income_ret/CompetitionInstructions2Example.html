In this scenario, participant A invested 16 tokens which corresponds to 40 % of the sum of all investments by the group members.
Therefore, participant A will get the higher wage in the next round with a probability of 40 %.

Making an investment can potentially increase your earnings in the following period but will reduce your earnings for the current period.
